About Me

The Mind Behind the Mission

My name is Pamilerin Adetunji Ajala, CEO of TeaJay Konsult Ltd — a company dedicated to building practical digital solutions for businesses, students and individuals across Africa.

I started my development journey with a passion for technology and a vision to make digital tools accessible to everyone. Every product I build carries that same mission — simple, powerful and built with purpose.
